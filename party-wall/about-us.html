Survey One – Your Trusted Partner in Party Wall Surveying and Property Solutions

At Survey One, we specialize in providing expert party wall surveying services, tailored to meet the unique needs of both residential and commercial property owners. Whether you're planning an extension, loft conversion, or any construction work that may affect shared walls, our team of RICS-regulated surveyors is here to guide you through the complexities of the Party Wall Act 1996, ensuring your project proceeds smoothly and within legal boundaries.

We understand that navigating property-related matters can be a daunting experience, especially when it comes to party wall disputes. Our goal is to simplify the process, offering professional, efficient, and reliable surveying services that you can trust. From serving party wall notices to resolving disputes and preparing party wall agreements, Survey One provides comprehensive support from start to finish.

Our Services

Survey One offers a comprehensive range of services, including:

  • Party Wall Surveys: Expert assessments to determine the condition of shared walls and prevent potential disputes.
  • Party Wall Notices: Assistance in properly serving the necessary notices to adjoining owners, ensuring compliance with the Party Wall Act.
  • Party Wall Agreements & Awards: Drafting legally binding documents to outline rights and responsibilities, resolving any disagreements that may arise.
  • Dispute Resolution: Mediation and expert advice to handle party wall disputes and maintain harmonious relationships between neighbors.
  • Schedules of Condition: Detailed documentation of a property’s condition before any construction, helping to avoid future liability for damages.

Our team also specializes in property surveying for homebuyers, structural surveys, and building surveys, providing a full spectrum of services to cater to your property needs.
